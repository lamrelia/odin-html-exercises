Air Fryer Meatloaf

Description

This juicy air fryer meatloaf is easy to make in a fraction of the time compared to cooking a meatloaf in the oven. Serve with your favorite sauce and mashed potatoes for a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Egg
  • Dried Bread Crumbs
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix the meat, onion, egg, breadcrumbs, and seasonings in a bowl.
  2. Transfer the mixture to small loaf pan, press in the mushrooms, and coat with oil.
  3. Roast until browned, let cool slightly, the cut into slices.
