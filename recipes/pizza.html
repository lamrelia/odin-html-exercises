Pizza

Description

This 2-ingredient pizza dough is fast and easy to make and tastes great! Greek yogurt is the secret ingredient and baking the crust at a higher temperature is the key to making this the perfect pizza. It doesn't get any easier than this folks.

Ingredients

    Self-rising flour Yogurt

Steps

    Mix Ingredients: Mix the flour and yogurt until a shaggy dough forms. Knead: Transfer to a flour dusted work surface, then knead for 8 to 10 minutes. Add more flour as needed if the dough becomes too sticky. Parbake: Grease the pizza pan with cooking spray. Spread the dough to the edges of a pan. Parbake the crust for 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from the oven, then add sauce and toppings. Finish Baking: Return the pizza to the oven and bake until the crust is lightly browned.
